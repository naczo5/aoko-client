*Closest Player* shows an on-screen panel that tracks the nearest player — their direction, exact distance, and equipment.

## Version support

1.8.9 · 1.21.x · 26.1

## Settings

This module is a single toggle.

## Usage notes

- The panel surfaces the closest player's direction relative to you, their precise distance, and visible gear.
- It pairs well with [Nametags](/aoko-client/modules/visual/nametags/) for situational awareness.
- The panel can be repositioned with the [HUD Editor](/aoko-client/modules/visual/hud-editor/).

:::tip
Useful for knowing when someone is approaching from behind in open PvP areas.
:::
